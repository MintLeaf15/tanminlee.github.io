Tan Min Lee's Portfolio

My name is Min Lee. Before transitioning to data analysis, I spent three years as a process engineer, where I analyzed data from various sources to identify the root cause of product failure and quality issues. It was during this time that I realized my love for analyzing data to find hidden insights and answers. After starting an online kefir business during the COVID-19 pandemic, I gained valuable experience in managing a business, online marketing, and promoting products. Since then, I have been teaching myself SQL, Python, and Tableau to further my interest in data analysis and have completed several projects that showcase my skills.

On my website, I feature some of my data analysis projects, which include interactive visualizations. Have fun in browsing through the content and thank you for visiting!

Nashville Housing Data Cleaning using SQL

This project focuses on the importance of data cleaning, a crucial step in any data analysis process. Using Microsoft SQL, we will perform data cleaning on a set of raw housing data in Nashville by identifying and correcting errors, removing duplicates, and addressing any other issues that may impact the accuracy and reliability of the data. This will allow us to draw valid conclusions and make informed decisions based on the clean data.
